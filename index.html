Wolfgang Maehr

product architect × beach ultimate player × humanist gypsy

Interaction Designer making digital products usable, delightful and meaningful around the globe for close to two decades. Believes in our agency and responsibility shaping our world and the path of this digital revolution towards a greater good. Fascinated by the interplay of societies, culture, technology and politics.

  • Head of Product and Co-Founder at SeaBoard, digital multi-currency wallet to financially empower seafarers, in Singapore.
  • Former Design and Engagement Lead at PebbleRoad, a management & design consultancy in Singapore.
  • Former Head of Design at Gumbuya, a digital platform start-up in Singapore.
  • Former co-founder of Extra Thought, a design consultancy in Singapore.
  • Former interaction designer at Opera, when they still made browsers.
  • Started and ran DestrActions, a monthly hands-on design meet-up.
  • Beach ultimate player and actively involved on the WFDF Board and chairing the Spirit of the Game Committee, and Singapore Beach Ultimate. Previously worked on Comms, IT Admin and Governance for India Ultimate.

Loves to move and explore new places, nature, cultures and people.
